Introduction

Accelerated by the tremendous increase in wearable camcorders such as GoPro cameras and Polaroid brands, action-camera captured videos (ACCVs) has been generated, published and spread explosively, becoming an indispensable part of todayâ€™s big data. As such, browsing and editing ACCVs is really a tedious job as many of the ACCVs are usually extremely unstructured and long-running. Therefore, video summarization, which produces a short summary of a fulllength video and ideally encapsulates its most informative parts, is becoming increasingly important for alleviating the problem of ACCVs browsing, editing and management.

As there are no available open ACCV datasets, we collect a new dataset from YouTube for video summarization on ACCVs. The dataset consists of 74 raw or little edited ACCVs mainly captured by HD GoPro cameras for 20 sports and travels related categories, each with a duration of 4 to 60 minutes.

Download

The video files or their YouTube links of the dataset can be obtained by sending a request email to us. Specifically, the researchers interested in ACCV Dataset should email to postmaster@accv-dataset.xyz (Email title: ACCV Dataset data request) and guarantee that all the data is used for research only. We will then send you the download instructions at our discretion.
